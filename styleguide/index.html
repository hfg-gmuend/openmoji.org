Grid

The Grid serves as orientation for the size of the emojis. If possible your emojis should stay inside the Grid, but for edge cases there are 4px of padding, that can be used.

basic forms

The four basic forms define the possible extents, aiming to visually balance every icon. They don't always have to be fully filled.

A special case are the “Emoji Faces”. They are placed inside a smaller circle to leave space for additions (such as tears or hands).

Examples

Basic Circles

The basic circles help to give a consistent look to the entire set. Parts of those basic circles should also be used for rounded corners or wavy forms that can be broken down into those circles.

Examples

Contour

The stroke settings are: 2px weight, round corners and ends. Two overlapping contours should have a minimum gap of 2px.

Open contour

The open contours give a nice dynamic look and should be used for overlapping lines or perspective.

Perspective

All objects should be displayed 2-dimensionally in a frontal view. If it is really necessary for a better differentiation you can use an orthographic projection, but this happens very rarely though.

Color

Please aim to use the primary colors only. The default is the lighter color. The darker version should be used primarily for shadows. In special cases, you can use the secondary colors (orange, violet, brown and pink) too.

Shadow

The lighter colour is used for the general fill, while the darker colour is for shading.

Typography

We provide a custom font for emojis which have to contain typographic elements, please use it for every text.

Topics

People

full body

People are depicted without clothing. The legs slightly run towards each other at the ends. Armes are displayed as a single line. Static people are portrayed from the front, moving people from the side. Any complexity of objects with a lot of overlapping forms should be reduced.

bust

The face is elliptical. There is no neck, but there is a gap between head and shoulders. The Eyes are in the middle of the head. For a neutral look, the mouth should aligned to the centre point of the eyes.

Hands

The thumb runs converges slightly at the end. The lower attachment of the hand is rounded. Fingers have a width of 7px.

Arrows

The arrow head is made of one basic form.

Arrows are doubled line for every emoji. For certain special cases e.g. icons for user interfaces, a single line is possible too

Buildings

Buildings are shown from the front with an entrance door. Details should be reduced strongly.

Animals and Plants

Plants are depicted organically and open. Stems should always be a single line. Animals with a big body are displayed from the side. The form should be organic, but not trivialized.

Vehicles

Vehicles should be deduced from the shapes of real world models to avoid a toyish look. Vehicles are depicted from the side. Tires are placed inside the bottom line.
